Commentary — Impact of Gold Cap

With gold capped at 3% SAA / 4% TAA, the strategy still outperformed the 60/40 benchmark by +2.8% ann. and all three IA Mixed sectors, but significantly underperformed the uncapped version (10.6% vs 14.8% ann.). The gold cap cost approximately 4.0% ann. in foregone returns by limiting exposure to the $1,800→$5,000/oz rally.

Freed weight was redistributed to: (1) Increased FI (+6%) with TIPS as inflation hedge and expanded EM debt for carry; (2) Broad commodities (+2%) partially replacing gold; (3) Absolute return (+5%) via Lumyna, Montlake, and Mygale replacing gold’s diversification benefit. With gold constrained, selection effect (+2.11%) now dominates allocation effect (+0.70%), making manager alpha the critical engine — 75% of total active return.
