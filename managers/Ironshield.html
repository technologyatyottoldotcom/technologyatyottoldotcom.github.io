Executive Summary

Ironshield Special Situations L1 is a London-based long/short opportunistic credit fund focused on event-driven, stressed and distressed European corporate credit, managed by David Nazar (CIO/Founder) since August 2007. The fund targets mispriced opportunities in actively traded bonds, loans, and CDS with event catalysts typically within 3–6 months.

Key Strengths

18-year live track record with 15 of 18 positive annual returns, +9.0% CAGR net.
Highly idiosyncratic returns — only 10.9% variance explained by HF factor indices.
~99% of portfolio liquidated within 2 weeks, rare for credit strategies.

Strategy, Edge & Process

Return Driver: Credit event premia from mispriced European corporate bonds/loans/CDS. Near-term catalysts — restructurings, refinancings, covenant triggers — typically within 3–6 months.

Edge: Informational and structural. Nazar's 30+ year network provides proprietary deal flow in under-researched European B/CCC credit where larger managers cannot deploy.

Process: Bottom-up fundamental, scenario-weighted probability framework. ~30 issuers / 46 positions. Net ~120%, gross ~162%. Top 5 = 73.6% NAV. Modified duration 1.2 years.
