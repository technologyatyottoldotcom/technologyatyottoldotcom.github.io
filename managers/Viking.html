Executive Summary

Andreas Halvorsen's (Tiger Cub) fundamental long/short equity fund. AUM ~$57bn ($18bn in private equity). Strong fundamental research across global equities with Tiger Management pedigree. Large positions in financials, tech, healthcare. Concentrated long book with diversified shorts. Consistent long-term compounder with occasional large drawdowns (2022).

Key Strengths

Tiger Cub pedigree; Halvorsen is among the most respected fundamental equity investors.
~$57bn AUM with both public equity L/S and private equity strategies.
Deep research culture; 26-year track record (founded 1999).
Strong sector diversification: financials, tech, healthcare.
Strong recovery from 2022 drawdown; +20% in both 2023 and 2024.

Key Risks

AUM at $57bn is very large for fundamental equity.
Halvorsen succession planning.
Private equity allocation changes liquidity profile.
