Executive Summary

Chris Hohn's concentrated, mostly-long equity fund with activist overlay. Founded 2004. Generated $18.9bn in gains for investors in 2025 — the single biggest annual haul by any hedge fund ever (LCH). 2023: +33%, 2024: +15%, 2025: ~+40% (estimated from $18.9bn gain on $44bn US equity portfolio). Three-year cumulative gains of $40bn. $68.4bn cumulative net gains since inception — 5th all-time. Highly concentrated: top holdings include GE Aerospace, MSCI, Visa, Alphabet, Canadian Pacific.
