Executive Summary

Contour Asset Management is a New York-based TMT-focused long/short equity manager. The strategy runs a low-net global L/S book predominantly in Technology, Media and Telecoms, targeting mispriced opportunities across large-, mid-, and small-cap names. The low-net construction is designed to deliver positive returns across market regimes while limiting downside — a differentiated offering versus the high-net sector specialists.

Key Strengths

Differentiated low-net TMT positioning — most sector specialists run materially net-long.
Sector specialist with deep tech research (hardware, software, internet, media, semiconductors).
Size advantage: smaller AUM enables mid/small-cap tech positions.
Structural dispersion in TMT post-AI emergence creates rich alpha opportunity set.

Key Risks

Smaller AUM brings firm-stability risk; redemption cascades possible.
TMT sector concentration = correlated losses in sector sell-offs (2022).
Low-net construction means giving up upside in strong tech rallies.
Short book in TMT painful during AI-driven multiple expansion since 2023.
Key-person risk: compact team.

Strategy, Edge & Process

Return Driver: Single-stock alpha in global Technology, Media, Telecoms. Long book on durable businesses; short book on disrupted incumbents.

Edge: Sector specialization with multi-cycle TMT PMs. Focus on second-order AI impact (beneficiaries vs. disrupted) rather than just NVIDIA/hyperscaler plays.

Construction: 40-70 longs / 30-60 shorts. Target net -10% to +30%. Gross 140-180%.

Vehicle & Terms: Cayman master-feeder. Monthly subs / quarterly redemptions. 1.5-2/20 with HWM.

Risk Management: Position-level sizing, sub-sector concentration limits, VaR monitoring.
