Executive Summary

DUNN Capital Management, founded by Bill Dunn in 1974, is one of the longest-running systematic trend-following managers globally. The WMA Institutional Program is a 100% systematic medium-to-long-term trend follower trading a diversified portfolio of financial, energy, metal and agricultural futures. The Institutional Program targets ~11.5% vol. Strategy AUM $1.69bn. Strong in 2022 trend regime (+26%) and 2024 (+9.8%); flat/negative in choppy periods (2023 -4.5%, 2020 -1.2%). Classic managed futures diversifier with crisis-alpha potential.

Key Strengths

50+ year firm track record (Bill Dunn founded 1974); CTA industry pioneer.
Purely systematic, transparent trend-following — no discretionary overlay or style drift.
Strong crisis alpha: +26% in 2022 (inflation/rates trend regime), +9.8% in 2024.
Very low correlation to equities (-0.18 vs S&P over 60 months) — genuine portfolio hedge.
Excellent terms: management fee as low as 0.00-0.10% on institutional class.

Key Risks

Trend-following structurally painful in choppy/mean-reverting regimes (2012, 2018, 2023).
60-month Sharpe 0.59 is mediocre on risk-adjusted basis.
Drawdowns can be deep and prolonged — 2012 was -20% with multi-year recovery.
Performance highly regime-dependent; correlated to other trend-followers (Barclay CTA 0.76).
Leverage and derivative-heavy.

Strategy, Edge & Process

Return Driver: Systematic medium-to-long-term trend capture across ~50 global futures markets — financial (rates, equities, FX), energy, metals, agriculturals.

Edge: Half-century of systematic trend-following R&D. Adaptive Risk Profile ("ARP") since 2013 targets stable risk across regimes. Crisis-alpha demonstrated 2008, 2022.

Construction: Volatility-targeted position sizing across all markets. Long/short per market via trend signals across multiple lookbacks. No sector tilts.

Vehicle & Terms: UCITS / equivalent offshore. Daily liquidity. Fees extremely competitive on institutional classes.

Risk Management: Systematic vol targeting, per-market risk caps, portfolio VaR, ARP dynamic risk adjustment. Daily transparency.
