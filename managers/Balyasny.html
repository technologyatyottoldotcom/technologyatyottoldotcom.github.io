Executive Summary

Dmitry Balyasny's multi-strategy platform with ~$31bn AUM. Diversified across equities, commodities, venture/growth, and macro. 2024: +15.2%, 2025: +16.7%. One of the strongest performers among second-tier multi-managers, consistently outperforming larger peers in 2025. The firm has grown rapidly from ~$12bn in 2020.

Key Strengths

Strong recent performance: +16.7% in 2025, outperforming Citadel and Millennium.
Diversified across equities, commodities, venture/growth, and macro trades.
Rapid but disciplined growth: $12bn (2020) → $31bn (2025).
Pod-based model with strict risk management and capital reallocation.

Key Risks

Rapid AUM growth may outstrip alpha capacity.
High total cost structure with passthrough fees.
Smaller track record than Citadel/Millennium — founded 2001.
Key-person risk on Dmitry Balyasny.
